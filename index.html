About Us

Holly & Oak Yarn Co. is a local yarn shop in South Bend’s Near Northwest neighborhood. Founded by indie dyer Briana, the shop offers a welcoming space for knitters, crocheters, and fiber artists of every skill level.

Our mission is built on four values: fellowship, creativity, inspiration, and community. We carry more than 11 brands and companies, including in-house hand-dyed yarns, and invite you to browse our collection in person and join our community of makers across Michiana.
